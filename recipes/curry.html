Paneer Butter Masala Curry

This is my personal recipe for butter paneer masala, a flavorful and spicy curry. This is a pretty simple dish to make requiring little time and only a few utensils.

Ingredients

  • 1 large white onion, diced
  • 1 tablespoon of minced garlic
  • 0.5 tablespoon of minced ginger
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 block of paneer cheese
  • 2 cups basmati or jasmine rice
  • Naan of your choosing
  • Spices:
    • Garam masala
    • Turmeric
    • Curry Powder
    • Cumin
    • Paprika
    • Chili Powder
    • Garlic Powder
    • Onion Powder

Steps

  1. Put a medium sized raised skillet or pot onto medium heat. The pot needs to be big enough for all the ingredients, so keep this in mind!
  2. Throw the onion, garlic, and ginger in the skillet with some oil and cook until a golden brown color. Season with the spices. For the seasoning, the amounts are up to you. More just means more heat, so start with a conservative amount if you are worried.
  3. After a few minutes, drain the can of diced tomatoes and throw them in the skillet with the other vegetables. Re-season.
  4. While the vegetables cook on the stove, start the rice. It will be eaten on the side with the curry, so this step can be done whenever.
  5. When the tomatoes are soft and tender, transfer the contents to a blender. Blend the vegetables until it is like a paste then put it back on the skillet. Slowly whisk in the heavy whipping cream.
  6. Once the curry is warm and bubbling some, put the paneer in the curry. You can have some variation with this step. I personally cube the paneer and keep them at about 1 by 1 inch cubes, but it is up to you.
  7. Serve the curry with rice and naan and enjoy!
